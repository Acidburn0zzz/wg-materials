IETF 77 - HTTPbis -08/-09 Changes Overview

Julian Reschke, greenbytes

History

  • -05: published November 2008 (during IETF 73)
  • -06: published March 2009 (before IETF 74)
  • -07: published July 2009 (before IETF 75)
  • -08: published October 2009 (before IETF 76)
  • -09: published March 2010 (before IETF 77)

Changes (1/3)

Part 1

  • Hard connection limit removed (http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/131)
  • Disallow control characters in quoted-pair (http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/194)
  • ABNF clarifications (http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/200 and http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/201)

Part 2

  • Clarify "Idempotency" (http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/27)
  • Clarify rules for determining what entities a response carries (http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/110)

Changes (2/3)

Part 3

  • Update to use RFC 4647 and RFC 5646 with respect to language tag handling (http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/13 and http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/181)
  • Update text about Content Negotiation (http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/81)

Part 4

  • Fixed leftover bug with respect to weak entity tags in requests other than GET (http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/116)

Changes (3/3)

Part 5

  • Clarifications for custom range units (http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/150)

Part 6

  • Clarifications about warn codes (http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/54 and http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/187)
  • Clarifications about Cache-Control directives (http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/169 and http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/197)
  • Header-specific canonicalization (http://trac.tools.ietf.org/wg/httpbis/trac/ticket/147)
